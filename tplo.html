ABOUT OLIVER'S EXPERIENCE

This was his second TPLO, but it was not simply a repeat of the first.

Oliver is an active Border Collie and Australian Cattle Dog mix who loves walking, swimming, retrieving, agility, and dock jumping. He had already experienced TPLO on his opposite rear leg, so I understood the general recovery burden. What I did not anticipate was how differently this recovery would be shaped by fireworks, stairs, cooling preferences, travel, and my own growing fear of letting him do too much.

The current published collection follows the documented period from the injury on May 21 through approximately July 16, 2026. It does not claim to be the complete medical record or the end of his rehabilitation story.

WHAT THIS RESOURCE DOES NOT CLAIM

Some questions still require verified records or veterinary direction.

I am deliberately leaving unresolved details unresolved rather than turning incomplete records into confident public guidance.
