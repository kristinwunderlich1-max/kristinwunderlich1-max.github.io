WHY THIS SITE EXISTS

I am not trying to be the expert. I am sharing what it was like to live through it.

I’m Kristin. I created this site because some of the most useful information I found came from other dog owners who were willing to describe the parts that do not fit neatly into a veterinary handout.

I document dates, routines, products, setbacks, questions, and small victories. My hope is that sharing those experiences will help another person feel more prepared, less alone, or simply reassured that their worry is understandable.

A NOTE TO THE PERSON READING AT 2:00 A.M.

You do not have to know everything tonight.

Take the next right step. Write down your questions. Call your veterinarian when something feels wrong. And remember that learning as you go does not mean you are failing your dog—it means you are caring deeply.
