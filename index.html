WHY THIS SITE EXISTS

Veterinary information explained the treatment. I still needed help with everyday life.

The hardest questions often started after the appointment: where the dog should sleep, how to manage stairs, what was worth buying, how to keep routines intact, and how to tell whether a change mattered.

The purpose of this site is to put those practical answers in one place without overstating what one dog's experience can prove.

Every resource should tell you

  • The answer to the question that brought you there
  • What Kristin directly observed
  • What came from the veterinary team
  • What changed after real use
  • What remains uncertain or dog-specific
  • What to consider next
